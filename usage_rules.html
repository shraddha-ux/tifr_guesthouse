Usage Rules

In order to provide efficient services to all our guests, a few simple instructions and regulations have been formulated by the management committee. Guests and hosts are requested to read and follow these guidelines.

Guesthouse Regulations

  • Ramanujan House is part of TIFR and is intended only for short and medium-term official visitors.
  • Field station staff visiting Colaba Campus for official work should send accommodation requests through their Faculty Member-in-Charge or Field Station In-Charge.
  • Guesthouse accommodation can be provided for a maximum period of three months.
  • Hosts must ensure that visitors are informed regarding bill settlement before departure.
  • Bookings for guests from India close 24 hours after the scheduled arrival time.
  • Bookings for guests arriving from abroad close 48 hours after the scheduled arrival time.
  • If a guest does not arrive within the above period, the booking will be cancelled and the room allotted to waitlisted guests.
  • Check-in time: 12:00 Noon.
  • Check-out time: 10:00 AM.
  • Please re-confirm your booking duration with reception after arrival.
  • Overstay beyond the approved booking period is not permitted.
  • Guests may leave baggage at reception after checkout if required.
  • In case of booking disputes, hosts should contact the Officer-in-Charge.
  • Unauthorized guests are not permitted to stay in guesthouse rooms.
  • Switch off ACs, lights and fans when leaving your room.
  • Alcoholic beverages are strictly prohibited.
  • Please store valuables in the electronic lockers provided.
  • Help us keep the guesthouse clean and pleasant for everyone.
  • Suggestions and complaints may be submitted through the feedback form or emailed to feedback.gh@tifr.res.in.

Noise Restrictions

  • The guesthouse is intended to provide a peaceful environment for all guests.
  • Loud television, music or other noise is not permitted, especially after 10:00 PM.
  • Please avoid gathering in corridors or disturbing other guests.
  • Maintain silence during late-night hours.
Quiet Hours: Guests are requested to maintain silence after 10:00 PM for the comfort of all residents.

Navy Nagar Regulations

  • TIFR is located within the Navy Nagar area and is subject to Indian Navy security regulations.
  • Photography is permitted only inside the TIFR Housing Complex.
  • Photography outside the campus and on Navy Nagar streets is strictly prohibited.
  • Security personnel may screen or question visitors entering Navy Nagar.
  • Guests are strongly advised to carry a valid photo ID at all times.
  • A temporary guesthouse ID card will be issued during check-in.
  • Please cooperate fully with security procedures and produce identification when requested.
Security Notice: Always carry your personal photo ID and guesthouse ID card while moving within Navy Nagar.
